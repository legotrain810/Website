Overview

Adobe Photoshop is the industry-standard software for digital image editing and graphic design. It offers a wide range of tools and features that allow you to create, enhance, and manipulate images with precision and creativity. Whether you're a professional photographer, graphic designer, or hobbyist, Photoshop empowers you to turn your ideas into stunning visuals.

Features

  • Advanced image retouching: Perfect your photos with powerful retouching tools.
  • Photo manipulation: Combine and transform images to unleash your creativity.
  • Graphic design capabilities: Create eye-catching graphics, posters, and more.
  • Layer-based editing: Work non-destructively by organizing content in layers.
  • Filters and effects: Apply artistic filters and effects to achieve unique looks.
  • Extensive brush library: Craft digital paintings with a wide variety of brushes.
